"les artistes souterrains" first exhibition :
"the underground artists"

on january 28th of 2024,
my friends from Bordeaux invite me to their first vernissage - art exhibition,
by their group "les artistes souterrains" (the underground artists).
which took place at Leonard's studio, one of the artists.

i didn't want to take any photos that evening,
i didn't bring a camera.

but my friend Gaspard handed me his old digital Lumix camera,
so i shoot my friends all the night,
especially Nathan, Maël, Gaspard and Leonard,
all artists from the exhibition except Maël.


here are the artists' instagram accounts :
@gowwwsha - @mael_h.2.0 - @karbon.graph - @_leonard_cgc_ - @aaaluca3
